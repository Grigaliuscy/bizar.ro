LUIS BIZARRØ18/SEP ’95 - SPCREATIVE+ DEVELOPER

About

I’m Luis Henrique Bizarro, I’m a Creative Developer based in São Paulo, Brazil specialized in Creative Coding for interactive projects like apps, games, installations and websites using web development technologies like WebGL, GLSL and JavaScript.

Bizarro literally means bizarre in brazilian Portuguese, which to me means something strikingly out of the ordinary.
I love having this surname and I try to embrace this adjective as part of my life and also my work.

I’m working at Active Theory as a Senior Interactive Developer.
I’m also currently the Most Awarded Brazilian at Awwwards and member of the Development Jury. In the past I’ve also collaborated with Antinomy and UNIT9.

During my career, I have had the opportunity to collaborate in challenging projects for renown international brands such as Nike, Google, Airbnb, Samsung, Lufthansa, Shell, Chevy Corvette, HBO, Cartoon Network, Discovery Kids, One Plus, GE, Citibank, among others.
